# v1.6-beta Multi-Zn Site Pair Screening Status

## Scope

v1.6-beta adds systematic screening of single-structure multi-Zn site combinations:

```text
examples/16_screen_multi_zn_combinations.py
```

Supported screening modes:

- `multi_q2b`
- `multi_q1`
- `q1_q2b_single_structure_mixture`
- `all`

Each candidate combination starts from the same pure C-S-H parent structure for the selected seed. The workflow does not use sequential mutation.

## Screening Rules

Each combination records:

- selected Si sites;
- motif types;
- PBC minimum-image Zn-Zn distance;
- planned hydroxylated O pairs;
- O/H pair conflict status;
- initial per-center Zn-O coordination;
- post-min per-center Zn-O coordination;
- failed center atom id and reason, when present.

Each candidate is independently generated, validated, run through LAMMPS read_check/run0/minimize when requested, reattached with CS-Info, and validated again after minimization.

Failed candidates do not stop screening. If no post-min valid candidate is found, the result is explicitly reported as `no_postmin_valid_candidate`.

## Post-Min Validation Quality

For multi-Zn screening, `postmin_valid` is a minimum-valid criterion: every Zn
center must have at least four O neighbors within the unchanged 2.5 Angstrom
Zn-O validation threshold. This does not imply that every retained center is an
ideal ZnO4 tetrahedron.

Coordination quality is reported separately:

- `ideal_fourfold`: all Zn centers have coordination exactly 4.
- `overcoordinated`: at least one Zn center has coordination greater than 4 and no Zn center is undercoordinated.
- `undercoordinated_failed`: at least one Zn center has coordination less than 4.
- `minimum_valid`: all Zn centers satisfy coordination >= 4 when no more specific quality label applies.

The best candidate JSON files only store candidates with `postmin_valid = true`
and the corresponding `valid_multi_*` post-min validation label. Undercoordinated
combinations such as 3;5 or 5;3 remain `failed_multi_zn_candidate` and are not
promoted as best candidates.

## Verified Screening Result

The current v1.6-beta screening run found:

- `multi_q2b`: 6 combinations screened, 6 post-min valid candidates in the final rerun. Best candidate `candidate_000002_multi_q2b` has `validation_postmin = valid_multi_q2b_zn_candidate`, per-center coordination `4;4`, `coordination_quality = ideal_fourfold`, and minimum Zn-Zn distance 16.4548 Angstrom.
- `multi_q1`: 10 combinations screened, 6 post-min valid candidates. Best candidate `candidate_000003_multi_q1` has `validation_postmin = valid_multi_q1_zn_candidate`, per-center coordination `5;6`, `coordination_quality = overcoordinated`, and minimum Zn-Zn distance 19.3095 Angstrom.
- `q1_q2b_single_structure_mixture`: 10 combinations screened, 8 post-min valid candidates. Best candidate `candidate_000009_q1_q2b_single_structure_mixture` has `validation_postmin = valid_multi_q1_q2b_zn_candidate`, per-center coordination `5;4`, `coordination_quality = overcoordinated`, and minimum Zn-Zn distance 14.2273 Angstrom.

Only the `multi_q2b` best candidate is ideal-fourfold. The `multi_q1` and
single-structure Q1+Q2b mixed-motif best candidates are minimum-valid but
overcoordinated; this version must not be described as producing ideal ZnO4
coordination for every multi-Zn motif.

## Non-Scope

This stage does not modify CementFF4-Zn parameters, does not relax the 2.5 Angstrom Zn-O threshold, does not change single-Zn validation semantics, does not enable the old `mixed_Q1_Q2b_Zn` site type, does not add finite-temperature MD, does not add `md_ready_candidate`, and does not claim final elastic constants or production mechanical properties.

Q1_Zn is still not added to the default `examples/07_run_quasistatic_mechanics.py` targets.
