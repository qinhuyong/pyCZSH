## Final Boundaries

v1.9 does not modify CementFF4-Zn parameters, does not relax the 2.5 Angstrom
Zn-O coordination threshold, does not change single-Zn or multi-Zn validation
semantics, does not enable the old `mixed_Q1_Q2b_Zn` site type, does not add
finite-temperature MD, does not introduce `md_ready_candidate`, does not claim
final elastic constants, does not claim production mechanical properties, and
does not add Q1_Zn to the default `examples/07_run_quasistatic_mechanics.py`
targets.

The default mechanics runner remains pure C-S-H and Q2b_Zn only. Q1_Zn and
multi-Zn mechanics remain opt-in.
